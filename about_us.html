Our story


Our team, BBY 21 decided to develop a game to help and educate users on how to properly recycle, and figure out which recycling bin an item belongs to. This can help reduce the environmental impact caused by improper waste composition by providing the user with proper recycling instructions.



We hope you enjoy our app, and learn something new about to recyling and categorizing trash.
