Why is recycling important?

- It conserves valuable resources and reduces the need for raw materials, which helps preserve natural habitats and biodiversity.
- Recycling minimizes the energy required to produce new products, leading to a decrease in greenhouse gas emissions and combating climate change.
- It reduces the strain on landfills, promoting sustainable waste management practices.
